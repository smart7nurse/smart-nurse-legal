Privacy Policy — Smart Nurse

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Smart Nurse ("we", "our", "the app") is a clinical reference and professional-development app for nurses and healthcare professionals in the Gulf region. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your choices.

⚠️ Clinical Disclaimer: All clinical tools in this app (ECG analysis, ventilator assistant, calculators, protocols) are for educational and reference purposes only. They do not replace clinical judgment or professional diagnosis. Do not enter real patient names or identifiable patient information into any field.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you first open the app, an anonymous account is created automatically. If you choose to create a full account, we collect your email address and name. This information is stored securely via Firebase Authentication.

Photos and Images

If you use the ECG AI Scanner feature, you may choose to upload a photo from your camera or gallery. This image is sent to Google Gemini AI for analysis. If you use the CV Builder, you may add a profile photo. Images are processed for the feature you requested and are not stored permanently on our servers.

Voice and Audio

The Interview Academy's Voice Interview feature uses your device microphone to capture your spoken answers. The audio is converted to text on your device using speech-to-text, and the resulting text may be sent to Google Gemini AI for evaluation. We do not store raw audio recordings.

Files and Documents

The Roster Scanner feature allows you to upload Excel files containing shift schedules. These files are processed locally on your device to generate your roster view and are not uploaded to our servers.

Salary Database Submissions

If you choose to submit a salary entry to the community database, we collect: hospital name, country, position, salary amount, currency, year, years of experience, nationality, and allowance information. This data is stored in Cloud Firestore and is visible to other app users as anonymous community data.

Usage and Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics to understand which features are used and how often, so we can improve the app. We also track in-app search queries to improve search results. This data is aggregated.

Crash and Diagnostic Data

We use Firebase Crashlytics to automatically receive technical reports if the app crashes. These reports may include device model, OS version, and app state at the time of the crash.

Device Identifiers and Advertising ID

The app displays banner advertisements powered by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect your device's Advertising ID to show relevant ads. This identifier is shared with Google's advertising network. You can reset or limit your Advertising ID in your device settings.

Push Notification Token

If you allow notifications, a device token is generated via Firebase Cloud Messaging so we can send you reminders (e.g. license expiry alerts, shift reminders). This token is not linked to your personal identity.

Information You Enter

This includes professional details (name, department, years of experience, certifications, salary goals) and values you enter into clinical tools (calculators, care plan builder, sedation assistant, wellbeing tracker). These are stored locally on your device using secure local storage.

2. Third-Party Services and AI

Google Gemini AI

Several features send data to Google Gemini AI for processing, including:

  • ECG Scanner — ECG images and clinical context
  • Interview Academy — spoken answer transcripts
  • Career Roadmap, Cover Letter, CV Builder — career-related text you enter
  • Ventilator Assistant — clinical parameters you enter

Data sent to Gemini is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. AI outputs are for educational reference only and may not be accurate.

Google Firebase

  • Firebase Authentication — manages anonymous and email-based accounts
  • Cloud Firestore — stores community salary data; personal data access is restricted to your own account
  • Firebase Analytics — aggregated usage statistics
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reporting
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — push notifications
  • Firebase App Check — protects against abuse

Google AdMob

The app displays banner advertisements. AdMob may use your Advertising ID to serve relevant ads. See Google Ads Privacy.

RapidAPI / Job Search

The job search feature uses the JSearch API via RapidAPI to retrieve job listings. Your search query and country preference are sent to this service. No personal account information is sent.

Open-Meteo

The weather widget uses the free Open-Meteo API with fixed coordinates for Dammam, Saudi Arabia. No personal location data is sent.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's features
  • To keep your saved data available across sessions
  • To send notifications you have opted into
  • To understand usage patterns and fix technical problems
  • To display relevant advertisements
  • To protect the app and its backend from abuse

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared with:

  • Google — Firebase services, AdMob advertising, Gemini AI processing
  • RapidAPI — job search queries only
  • Other app users — salary submissions you voluntarily submit to the community database

5. Data Storage and Security

Most preferences and logs are stored locally on your device. Account-linked data in Cloud Firestore is protected by security rules restricting access to your own authenticated account. All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time using the link below or by contacting us. Uninstalling the app removes locally stored data from your device.

→ Request Account or Data Deletion

7. Children's Privacy

Smart Nurse is a professional tool intended for nurses, nursing students, and healthcare professionals. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Your Choices

  • You can use most features anonymously without providing an email
  • You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time
  • You can reset or limit your Advertising ID in Android Settings → Privacy → Ads
  • You can request a copy or deletion of your data by contacting us

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
