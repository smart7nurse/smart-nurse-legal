Privacy Policy — Smart Nurse

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Smart Nurse ("we", "our", "the app") is a clinical reference and professional-development app for nurses. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you first open the app, an anonymous account is created automatically so your data (preferences, saved logs, professional records) can be kept on your device and, if you choose, linked to an email address. If you create an account with an email and password, we store that email address.

Usage data. We use Firebase Analytics to understand which features are used, how often, and for how long, so we can improve the app. This data is aggregated and not tied to your name.

Crash and diagnostic data. We use Firebase Crashlytics to automatically receive technical reports if the app crashes or encounters an error, so we can fix bugs. These reports may include device model, OS version, and the app state at the time of the crash.

Push notification token. If you allow notifications, a device token is generated so we can send you reminders (e.g. license expiry, shift reminders). This token is not linked to your identity beyond your device.

Information you enter into the app. This includes professional details you choose to add (name, department, years of experience, certifications), and reference values you enter into clinical tools (calculators, care plan builder, sedation assistant, wellbeing tracker). These fields are designed for clinical reference and self-tracking purposes. Do not enter real patient names or any information that could identify a specific patient — all clinical tools in this app are reference and calculation aids for the nurse's own use, not a patient record system.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's features
  • To keep your saved data (professional records, preferences, tracked logs) available across sessions
  • To send you notifications you have opted into (e.g. expiry reminders, shift reminders)
  • To understand usage patterns and fix technical problems
  • To protect the app and its backend from abuse (via Firebase App Check)

3. Third-Party Services

Smart Nurse uses Google Firebase for backend infrastructure. The specific Firebase services in use are:

  • Firebase Authentication — manages your account (anonymous and/or email-based)
  • Cloud Firestore — stores your account-linked data securely; access is restricted so only your own signed-in account can read or write your data
  • Firebase Analytics — aggregated usage statistics
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash and error reporting
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — push notifications
  • Firebase App Check — verifies requests come from our genuine app, protecting against abuse

These services are provided by Google and are governed additionally by Google's Firebase privacy terms. We do not sell your data to third parties or use it for advertising.

4. Data Storage and Security

Most of your personal preferences and logs are stored locally on your device. Account-linked data stored in Cloud Firestore is protected by security rules that restrict access to your own authenticated account only. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. Uninstalling the app removes locally stored data from your device, but data already linked to your account in Firestore will remain until you request deletion.

6. Children's Privacy

Smart Nurse is a professional tool intended for nurses, nursing students, and healthcare professionals. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your country).

7. Your Choices

  • You can use most features anonymously without providing an email
  • You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time
  • You can request a copy or deletion of your data by contacting us

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
