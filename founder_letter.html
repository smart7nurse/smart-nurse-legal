A Letter From the Founder

Smart Nurse

To every nurse reading this —

Thank you for trusting Smart Nurse with something as important as your shift.

Why This App Exists

I built Smart Nurse because I am a nurse. I have stood at the bedside at 3 a.m. trying to remember a drug compatibility, searching for a protocol on my phone between call bells, and wishing there was one place that just had everything — without ads shouting at me, without a hundred taps to find one number.

This app started as a personal notebook. It grew because other nurses asked for a copy. It kept growing because every one of you told me what was missing.

What I Promise You

  • I will keep building this with input from real nurses, not just my own assumptions.
  • I will always tell you clearly when something is a reference tool and not a replacement for your hospital's protocol or your own clinical judgment.
  • I will protect your data the way I would want mine protected.
  • I will keep the core of this app accessible, because the nurses who need it most are often the busiest and most exhausted ones on the floor.

What I Ask of You

Tell me when something is wrong. Tell me when a drug concentration doesn't match your unit's practice. Tell me when a feature is confusing. This app gets better because nurses like you take thirty seconds to send feedback instead of just closing the app.

And if Smart Nurse saved you five minutes during a hard shift, or helped you double-check a dose you weren't sure about — that is exactly why I built it.

With respect for the work you do every single shift,
The Smart Nurse Team
