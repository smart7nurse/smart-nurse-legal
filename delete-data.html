Data Deletion Request — Smart Nurse Last updated: July 19, 2026 Smart Nurse, developed by smart7nurse, lets you request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. This page explains how. How to Request Deletion Send an email to smart7nurse@gmail.com from the email address associated with your Smart Nurse account (if you created one). Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request". Include the email address linked to your account so we can locate it. If you only used the app anonymously (without creating an account), let us know the approximate date you used the app and your device type instead. We will confirm your request and complete deletion within a reasonable time, generally within 30 days. What Gets Deleted Your account (email address, authentication record) Any account-linked data stored in our backend (Cloud Firestore) What May Be Retained Anonymous salary database entries: entries submitted to the salary-sharing feature are collected anonymously and are not linked to your account or identity in any way, so they cannot be located or deleted via an account-deletion request. No entry in that database contains your name, email, or any identifying information. Aggregated analytics and crash reports: usage statistics and crash diagnostics (via Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics) are collected in aggregate/anonymized form and are not tied to your personal identity, so they are not individually deletable. Data already required to be retained by law, if any, for the minimum period required. Uninstalling the App Uninstalling Smart Nurse removes all data stored locally on your device immediately. It does not, by itself, delete any account-linked data stored in our backend — please follow the steps above for that. Contact us: Email: smart7nurse@gmail.com
